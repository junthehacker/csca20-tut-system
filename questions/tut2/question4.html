Inputs

driver’s license (String - length 16), age (integer 0-100), years of driving (integer 0-100), has_course (boolean), VIN (String)

Outputs

monthly fee (dollar value)

Expected Behaviour

Return a quote of a monthly fee for car insurance

- Driver with a G2 license has the base fee of $40 and a G license has a base fee of $30

- For every past accident that the car has been involved, add $5

- Consider the driver’s demerit points, add $4 per demerit point

- Drivers with a driving course certificate or with more than 5 years driving will get a 10% discount

- Drivers 25 or older will get a 5% discount.
